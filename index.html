About

I am a Ph.D. student in the George W. Woodruff School of Mechanical Engineering at Georgia Tech, working with Prof. Seung-Kyum Choi.

My research sits between computer vision, industrial AI, and mechanical systems. I am especially interested in the evidence that makes a visual decision trustworthy: CAD priors, normal-reference data, calibrated feature spaces, and test-time verification.
